Payments & Security

// Last updated: January 2025

Your security matters to us. This page explains how payments are processed, which methods we accept and the measures we take to protect your data.

1. Secure payment processing

Payments are processed by Stripe, a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment provider. Your card details are entered in a secure environment and are never stored on Vanguard's servers.

2. Accepted payment methods

We accept major credit and debit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express and Elo, subject to availability in your country.

3. Encryption

All data transmitted between your device and our services is protected with industry-standard TLS encryption. Sensitive payment data is tokenized by our payment provider.

4. Statement descriptor

Charges may appear on your statement under the name Vanguard Group or the name of its payment processor. If you do not recognize a charge, please contact us before disputing it so we can assist you quickly.

5. Fraud prevention

We and our payment provider use fraud-detection tools to protect customers, including measures against unauthorized "card testing". Transactions flagged as high risk may require additional verification or be declined.

6. Currencies and taxes

Prices are displayed at checkout in Brazilian Real (R$) or Euro (€), shown before you confirm your purchase. Depending on your location, applicable taxes may be added, and your bank may apply currency-conversion fees for international transactions.

7. Refunds via Stripe

Stripe does not issue refunds on behalf of businesses. To request a refund, please contact Vanguard Group directly; Stripe only processes the payment. See our Refund & Returns policy for details.

8. Checkout acceptance

At checkout you confirm acceptance of our Terms of Use and Privacy Policy before completing the purchase.

9. Billing questions and disputes

For billing questions, refunds or to report an unrecognized charge, contact support@vanguardgroup.store or call +55 (17) 99204-4283.

10. Data protection

For details about how we handle personal data, please review our Privacy Policy.
