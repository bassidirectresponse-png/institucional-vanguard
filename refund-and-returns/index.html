Refund & Returns

// Last updated: January 2025

We want you to be confident in your purchase. This policy explains when and how you can request a refund for digital products and subscriptions provided by Vanguard Group.

1. Digital products

Our services are delivered digitally as access to PWA applications and personalized plans. Because access is granted immediately, refunds are subject to the conditions described below and to the consumer-protection laws applicable in your jurisdiction.

2. Right of withdrawal (7 days)

In accordance with Article 49 of the Brazilian Consumer Protection Code (CDC), you may request a refund within 7 days of your purchase. Where a longer statutory withdrawal period applies in your jurisdiction, we honor it.

3. Eligibility

  • The request is made within the applicable refund window;
  • The purchase was made through our official channels;
  • The request is not based on a violation of our Terms of Use.

4. Non-refundable situations

  • Requests made after the applicable refund window has expired;
  • Accounts suspended or terminated for breach of our Terms;
  • Promotional or clearly non-refundable items, where lawful and disclosed at purchase.

5. How to request a refund

Email support@vanguardgroup.store with your order details and the reason for the request. Our team reviews each request individually and may ask for additional information.

6. Subscriptions

You can cancel a subscription at any time to prevent future charges. Cancellation stops upcoming renewals; access typically remains available until the end of the current billing period.

7. Processing time

Once approved, refunds are issued to the original payment method. Depending on your bank or card issuer, the amount may take several business days to appear on your statement.

8. Digital products & returns

Our products are digital, so there is no physical item to return. "Return" means revoking access to the corresponding plan or application once a refund is approved.

9. Payment processor (Stripe)

Stripe does not issue refunds on behalf of businesses. To request a refund, please contact Vanguard Group directly at support@vanguardgroup.store; Stripe only processes the payment.

10. Contact

Questions about refunds can be sent to support@vanguardgroup.store or through our Contact page.
