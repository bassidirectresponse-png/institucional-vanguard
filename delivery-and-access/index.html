Delivery & Access

// Last updated: January 2025

Vanguard Group provides digital products. This page explains how access is delivered after purchase, the requirements to use our applications and what to do if you have access issues.

1. Digital delivery

Our products are 100% digital. There is no physical shipment. After a successful purchase, access to the corresponding plan and PWA application is granted to your account.

2. Delivery time

Access is typically provisioned immediately after payment confirmation. In rare cases where additional verification is required, provisioning may take a little longer, and we will keep you informed.

3. How you receive access

  • A confirmation is sent to the email address associated with your purchase;
  • You sign in to your account to open the PWA application;
  • Your personalized plan becomes available within the app.

4. System requirements

As a Progressive Web App, our application runs in modern browsers on most devices (smartphone, tablet or desktop) and requires an internet connection for setup. Once loaded, certain features may be available offline.

5. Access issues

If you do not receive your access confirmation or cannot sign in, please check your spam folder first, then contact support@vanguardgroup.store or call +55 (17) 99204-4283. We will help restore access as quickly as possible.

6. Duration of access

Access duration depends on the plan or subscription purchased and is described at checkout. For subscriptions, access continues while the subscription remains active.

7. Related policies

For payment details see Payments & Security, and for cancellations and refunds see Refund & Returns.

8. Contact

Questions about delivery or access can be sent to support@vanguardgroup.store or through our Contact page.
